Growth In Greenville

More development is coming to one of the busiest sections of Woodruff Road, with a new hotel planned on Carolina Point Parkway. International hotel chain Choice Hotels announced Wednesday that it plans to open a 125-room Cambria brand hotel at 135 Carolina Point Parkway, near the Whole Foods shopping center off Woodruff Road. Construction is expected to begin later this year and it is projected to open in October 2019. Cambria is the company's upscale brand. Other Choice brands include Sleep Inn, Comfort Inn and Clarion.
