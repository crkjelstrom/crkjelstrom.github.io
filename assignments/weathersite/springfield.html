The Stars of Springfield

At the top of the key, Tyem Freeman lobs the ball toward the basket. He is usually on the receiving end of alley-oops — being one of the most athletic high school players in the area — but this time, he was passing it to his teammate at The Courts. Jared Ridder, a former star at Kickapoo, jumps up, catches the ball and slams it in. The two already know they're going to be teammates for the 2019-20 season at Missouri State, but they're going to need to work on their summer league point guard to keep together.
